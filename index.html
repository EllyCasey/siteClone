Honey Bees: Essential Pollinators for Global Food Security

Honey bees play a critical role in pollinating approximately one-third of the crops we consume, including fruits, vegetables, and nuts, thereby ensuring food diversity and nutrition. Their decline due to factors such as pesticides and habitat loss poses a significant threat to agricultural productivity and biodiversity worldwide.
